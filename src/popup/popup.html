    🧩 Download every file and combine the chunks in order (Audio 1, 2, 3 …) to get the complete voiceover.

    🎬 How to get the video
    1. Add your script and choose your avatar & voice.
    2. Open the Audio tab and play / generate the voice so the audio finishes rendering.
    3. Once the voice is ready, render the scene by clicking the Render Scene button so the video finishes rendering.
    4. Come back here and click Fetch Video.

    ⚠️ The scene must be fully rendered first — otherwise no video will appear yet.
